Get the app

Install it on your phone

It's a web app — no app store needed. Just add it to your home screen and open it like any app.

🤖 Android (Chrome)

  1. Open app.musingswithdeva.com
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right)
  3. Tap Install app / Add to Home screen
  4. Open Deva's Classes from your home screen

🍎 iPhone (Safari)

  1. Open app.musingswithdeva.com in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (□↑)
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen
  4. Open Deva's Classes from your home screen

Quick start

Guide for students

Everything you'll do in the app, start to finish.

1

Log in

Open the app and enter the User ID and password your teacher gave you. (No email needed.)

2

Book a class

Tap Book a Class → pick a date → tap a free time slot → choose the duration → confirm. A free slot is booked instantly; a busy one is sent to your teacher to approve.

3

Reschedule or cancel

In My Classes, tap Reschedule to request a new time (your teacher confirms it), or Cancel if you can't attend — your teacher is notified automatically.

4

Mark a class done

After a lesson, tap Mark Done to move it to your Completed list.

5

Pay your fees

Open Fees → Submit Payment → enter the amount, choose UPI / bank / cash, select the month(s), and upload a receipt screenshot. It shows Pending until your teacher confirms it.

6

Stay updated

The 🔔 bell shows you when a class is confirmed, a reschedule is approved, or a payment is accepted.

Open the app and log in with the details your teacher shared.
